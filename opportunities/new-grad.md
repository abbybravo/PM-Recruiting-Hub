## Notes for New Grad Applicants

- Many "New Grad" postings accept candidates within 0–6 months of graduation — check each posting's specific eligibility window.
- New grad recruiting for PM roles is typically **more competitive and lower-volume** than internship recruiting — internships remain the strongest pipeline into full-time PM roles.
- If you completed a PM internship at a company, ask your manager or recruiter about full-time conversion timelines before applying externally.

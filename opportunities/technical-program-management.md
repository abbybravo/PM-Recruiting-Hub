## What TPM Involves

- Coordinating complex, multi-team technical programs and launches
- Identifying and mitigating cross-team risks and dependencies
- Translating technical detail into clear status updates for stakeholders
- Often requires stronger technical fluency than traditional PM roles, making it a natural fit for engineering majors

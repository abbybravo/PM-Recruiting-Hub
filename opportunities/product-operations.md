## What Product Ops Involves

- Supporting product launches with cross-functional coordination
- Building and maintaining product metrics dashboards
- Improving internal processes between product, engineering, and go-to-market teams
- Often a strong entry point into PM for candidates from operations, business, or analytics backgrounds

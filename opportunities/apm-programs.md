# Associate Product Manager (APM) Programs

APM (and equivalent rotational PM) programs are structured, cohort-based full-time programs designed for early-career hires, typically new grads or those with 0–2 years of experience. Most recruit heavily in the fall for the following year's start date.

See the [status legend](../README.md#-status-legend) for symbol meanings.

## About APM Programs

- Typically 1–2 year rotational programs across 2–3 product teams.
- Often include dedicated mentorship, structured onboarding, and cohort-based community.
- Highly competitive — usually a handful of interviews including product sense, execution, and behavioral rounds. See [Interview Resources](../resources/interview-resources.md).
- Most programs recruit primarily from new grads, though some accept rising seniors for a "APM Intern" or "APM University" pathway.
